## Audit Scorecard

Score 1-5:

- Market pain.
- Purchasing power.
- Reachability.
- Avatar specificity.
- Dream outcome clarity.
- Mechanism differentiation.
- Proof strength.
- Time to first win.
- Effort reduction.
- Offer stack.
- Guarantee/risk reversal.
- Scarcity/urgency ethics.
- CTA clarity.
- Follow-up system.
- Unit economics.

After scoring, list:

- Biggest bottleneck.
- Fastest fix.
- Highest leverage fix.
- Risk if unchanged.
- Next 7 days.
- Next 30 days.

## Diagnosis Workflow

1. Restate the current offer/funnel.
2. Identify what the buyer must believe to buy.
3. Mark which beliefs are unsupported.
4. Tie each gap to value equation drivers.
5. Recommend fixes in priority order.
6. Produce revised copy or assets when requested.

Belief categories:

- "This is for me."
- "This solves the problem I care about."
- "This works."
- "This will work for me."
- "I can do this."
- "The price is worth it."
- "I should act now."

## Roadmaps

Default 30-day roadmap:

Week 1:

- Avatar and problem research.
- Offer diagnosis.
- Proof collection.
- First revised promise.

Week 2:

- Offer stack.
- Lead magnet.
- Sales page or VSL draft.
- Follow-up sequence.

Week 3:

- Warm outreach.
- Content and ads tests.
- Sales calls.
- Objection collection.

Week 4:

- Iterate offer/copy.
- Add referral ask.
- Build partner list.
- Decide next channel to scale.

## Output Formats

For copy generation:

- Strategy notes.
- Final copy.
- Variations.
- Assumptions/placeholders.
- Test plan.

For audits:

- Scorecard.
- Findings by severity.
- Fixes by priority.
- Revised copy examples.
- Metrics to watch.

For offers:

- One-line offer.
- Offer stack.
- Price framing.
- Bonuses.
- Guarantee.
- Scarcity/urgency.
- Names.
- CTA.

## Compliance and Ethics

Never fabricate:

- Revenue.
- Testimonials.
- Case studies.
- Scarcity.
- Credentials.
- Legal, medical, or financial outcomes.

Use cautious wording for regulated spaces:

- "Designed to help..."
- "May help..."
- "Based on..."
- "Results vary..."
- "When you complete..."

Prefer "capacity-based scarcity" over fake countdowns.

## Final Quality Pass

Before finalizing, verify:

- The avatar is specific.
- The result is concrete.
- The mechanism is clear.
- The copy has proof or proof placeholders.
- Every bonus has a job.
- The guarantee is believable.
- Urgency/scarcity is ethical.
- The CTA is explicit.
- The output is usable without more explanation.
