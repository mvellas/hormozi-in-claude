## VSL Structure

Default VSL:

1. Pattern interrupt and avatar callout.
2. Big promise.
3. Credibility and reason to listen.
4. Problem reframe.
5. Failed alternatives.
6. New mechanism.
7. Steps/framework.
8. Proof.
9. Offer reveal.
10. Bonus stack.
11. Guarantee/risk reversal.
12. Scarcity/urgency.
13. CTA and next steps.
14. Objection handling.
15. Final CTA.

## Script Beats

Hook:

```text
If you are [avatar] and [pain], this will show you how to [result] without [sacrifice].
```

Credibility:

```text
We discovered this after [credible experience/proof]. The pattern was simple: [insight].
```

Problem reframe:

```text
The reason [desired result] has been hard is not [false cause]. It is [true constraint].
```

Mechanism:

```text
That is why we use [mechanism], a [brief explanation] that helps [avatar] [result].
```

Offer reveal:

```text
To help you implement this, we built [offer name].
```

CTA:

```text
Click below to [action]. You will [what happens next]. We can only handle [capacity limit] because [real reason].
```

## Slide Outline

1. Title promise.
2. Who this is for.
3. The expensive problem.
4. Why common fixes fail.
5. The new mechanism.
6. The 3-5 step framework.
7. Proof/case study.
8. Offer name.
9. What is included.
10. Bonuses.
11. Guarantee.
12. Scarcity/urgency.
13. CTA.
14. FAQ/objections.
15. Final CTA.

## CTA Sequence

Use multiple CTAs after the offer reveal:

- Soft CTA: "If this already sounds like the right fit..."
- Main CTA: "Click below to apply/book/buy..."
- Urgency CTA: "Do it before [deadline]..."
- Risk reversal CTA: "And because [guarantee]..."
- Final CTA: "The next step is simple..."

## Variants

Short VSL, 3-5 minutes:

- Hook, problem, mechanism, offer, proof, CTA.

Webinar VSL, 30-60 minutes:

- Add education, stories, case studies, deeper objections, and extended stack.

Video ad script, 30-90 seconds:

- Callout, pain, result, proof/mechanism, CTA.

## Editing Checklist

- Does the first 10 seconds create relevance?
- Is the problem reframed?
- Is the mechanism memorable?
- Does proof appear before or near the offer?
- Does the CTA explain what happens after the click?
- Are claims compliant and supported?
- Is urgency real?
