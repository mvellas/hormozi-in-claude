## Sales Page Structure

Use this default structure:

1. Hero: avatar, result, mechanism, CTA.
2. Problem: name the pain and cost of inaction.
3. Failed alternatives: explain why common solutions fail.
4. Mechanism: introduce the new way.
5. Offer stack: show what is included.
6. Proof: testimonials, case studies, demonstrations, credentials.
7. Bonuses: remove objections.
8. Guarantee: reduce risk.
9. Scarcity/urgency: give an ethical reason to act.
10. FAQ/objections: answer buying friction.
11. Final CTA: repeat promise and next step.

## Section Prompts

Hero:

- Who is this for?
- What result do they want?
- What sacrifice do they want to avoid?
- What mechanism makes this different?

Problem:

- What is happening now?
- What does it cost?
- Why does it keep happening?
- What have they already tried?

Mechanism:

- What insight changes the game?
- Why did old methods fail?
- What are the steps of the new method?

Proof:

- Who achieved the result?
- What changed?
- Under what conditions?
- What proof can be shown without exaggeration?

## Copy Blocks

Hero formula:

```text
[Avatar]: Get [specific result] in [timeframe/context] without [common sacrifice] using [mechanism].
```

Subheadline formula:

```text
Built for [avatar] who are tired of [failed alternative] and want [dream outcome] with [reduced effort/risk].
```

Problem opener:

```text
If [current pain] keeps happening, the problem probably is not [common false cause]. It is [deeper mechanism/problem].
```

Mechanism intro:

```text
The difference is [mechanism]. Instead of [old way], you [new way], so [result].
```

Offer stack:

```text
Here is what you get:
- [Component]: [benefit]
- [Component]: [benefit]
- [Component]: [benefit]
```

FAQ answer:

```text
Short answer: [direct answer].
Why: [reason].
What this means for you: [buyer implication].
```

## CTAs

Match CTA to funnel temperature:

- Cold: get diagnosis, download lead magnet, watch training.
- Warm: book strategy call, request audit, start application.
- Hot: buy now, reserve seat, enroll before deadline.

CTA examples:

```text
Get the free diagnosis
Book your offer audit
Reserve one of [capacity limit] implementation spots
Start the [offer name] application
```

## Page Variants

Short page:

- Hero.
- Problem.
- Mechanism.
- Offer.
- Proof.
- CTA.
- FAQ.

Long page:

- Add story, deep objection handling, comparison table, case studies, guarantee, bonuses, and final urgency.

Application page:

- Emphasize qualification, scarcity, fit, and what happens after applying.

## Quality Checklist

- Does the first screen call out the right buyer?
- Is the result concrete?
- Is the mechanism differentiated?
- Is the offer stack clear?
- Is proof specific or honestly marked as a placeholder?
- Are bonuses tied to objections?
- Is scarcity real?
- Is there one primary CTA?
- Does the copy avoid fake claims?
